If you live in Clinton Township or Macomb Township, the local housing market over the last twelve months looked like this: 1,172 homes closed, 177 are on the market right now, 138 are pending, and the median home sold for $349,900 in a median of 15 days at 99.4% of asking. This isn't an algorithm guessing. It's the actual MLS data for the Chippewa Valley Schools district.

If you've been reading national headlines about "the slowdown," that's not what's happening here. Houses are selling fast, sellers are getting essentially their full asking price, and the workhorse mid-tier ($300K to $700K homes) accounts for 63% of every closing in the district. Median time on market is 15 days, faster than most Oakland County districts. The story for buyers, sellers, and homeowners is different from what you'd guess from generic market chatter. What Chippewa Valley families lean into

Frequently asked questions

What is the median home price in Chippewa Valley Schools?

$349,900 over the last 12 months, based on 1,172 closed transactions. Median by ZIP: $250K in 48036 (Clinton Township entry tier), $305K in 48038 (Clinton Township main), $449K in 48044 (Macomb Township main), and $507K in 48042 (Macomb Township upper-mid). The full range covers $50,000 at the entry end up to $925,000 at the high end.

How fast are homes selling right now?

15 days on market median. The list-to-sale ratio is 99.4%, meaning sellers are getting essentially full asking price. Five of the eight most recent over-ask closings sold within a week of listing. This is one of the fastest markets in Southeast Michigan right now.

How many homes are for sale in Chippewa Valley right now?

177 active listings as of May 19, 2026. Add 138 pending sales and you have 315 homes currently in motion. Active inventory is concentrated in the $300K-$700K mid-tier with limited supply at the entry level and very few luxury listings above $700K.

What ZIP codes are in the Chippewa Valley Schools district?

The four primary ZIPs are 48036 (Clinton Township entry tier), 48038 (Clinton Township main), 48042 (Macomb Township upper-mid), and 48044 (Macomb Township main). Tiny edges of 48039 and other neighboring ZIPs touch the district boundary, but those four ZIPs cover more than 99% of district transaction volume.

Is now a good time to buy in Chippewa Valley?

The market is balanced toward sellers but not as extreme as 2021-2022. Buyers can win, but you need a fully processed pre-approval, a tight offer, and a willingness to move fast on the right home. 15-day median DOM means you have days, not weeks, to decide. The flip side: inventory exists at 177 active homes, so you are not facing zero choices.
