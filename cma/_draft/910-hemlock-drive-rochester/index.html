Rochester 48307 is running hot. The median sale price is sitting around $585K, up nearly 15% from a year ago, and price-per-square-foot has jumped over 25% year-over-year to $279. Homes are going under contract in an average of 19 days — which, in a market like this, means well-priced homes are effectively gone in two weekends.

The trend line is pointing up and hasn't shown meaningful signs of softening through Q2 2026. Low inventory continues to be the driver — there simply aren't enough homes in desirable Rochester zip codes to meet buyer demand. Until that changes, sellers hold the leverage. The four comps in this analysis all sold in 8–14 days, well under the market average.
