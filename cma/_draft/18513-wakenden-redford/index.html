The adjusted comp math produces a weighted value around $191,000 — but the recommendation is to launch at $185,000. The double lot and fenced yard are genuine selling points that photos need to show clearly. Price it right and show it right, and you won't need to negotiate down from anything.

The biggest lever you have right now is timing. Every week the home sits occupied is a week of market time you're not capturing in a segment that's moving fast. Once Tom is out and the place is clean, you're ready to go — and this market will reward you for moving decisively.
